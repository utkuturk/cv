## Projects

### The Role of Working Memory in Sentence Processing
**Role**: Researcher

Analysis of behavioral data from psycholinguistic experiments testing topics including response bias, grammaticality illusions, shallow processing, discourse effects. (R, Stan, brms, tidyverse, IbexFarm, git, OpenSesame, EyeLink)

### Deep Learning Based Turkish Dependency Parsing
**Role**: Researcher

Lead annotator in Universal Dependencies project for Turkish. Creating annotation guidelines for Turkish in the Universal Dependencies framework. (Python, CoNLL-U, udpipe, git, bash, Dependency Parsing, LSTMs, transformer)

### Greek and Turkish in Contact: Morphosyntactic Change and its Sociological Causation
**Role**: Researcher

Statistical analysis of the corpus and geospatial data. Sociolinguistic annotation of the first Cappadocian Greek treebank. (CoNLL-U, udpipe, tidyverse, geospatial analysis)
